RESEARCH SNAPSHOT

Shiori is not competing on speed or audit readiness.

Automation sells speed

Workflow consultants usually ask what can be automated, ranked by time saved or ROI.

Governance sells audit readiness

Formal AI governance asks whether controls, evidence, and oversight are ready for review.

Shiori focuses on what stays yours

Judgment, voice, review, meaning, and ownership are treated as design boundaries.
