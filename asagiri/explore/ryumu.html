The day has not yet
condensed into shape.

Asagiri runs every morning before the world decides what it is. Ten possibilities, born from the night's accumulated signal, drift across the surface like vapour finding a window. Most will burn off by mid-morning. The few that remain are the ones worth touching.

Three surfaces

engine

Engine

Thin orchestrator over four CLI agents. Local SearXNG for context. No vendor SDK on the hot path.

webapp

Webapp

A Next.js dashboard for browsing the day's ideas. Ritual, Kanban, Territory, Settings.

widget

Sasagani widget

A 7 KB drop-in fragment-capture button. Always docked, never blocking the page beneath it.
