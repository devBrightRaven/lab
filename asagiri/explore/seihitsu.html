Asagiri 朝霧 Direction A · 静謐

朝霧は、
一日が形を決める前の、
静かな余白。

The mist before the day decides its shape. Asagiri is a daily research engine — but its surface is the page after the engine has finished, when ten possibilities sit quietly waiting to be considered. Paper, ink, breath, space.

三つの面 / Three surfaces

一
engine

Engine

Thin orchestrator over four CLI agents. No vendor SDK lock-in. Search and generation kept on separate failure surfaces.

二
webapp

Webapp

A Next.js dashboard for browsing the day's ideas. Ritual, Kanban, Territory, Settings — same vocabulary across them all.

三
widget

Sasagani widget

A 7 KB drop-in fragment-capture button. Always docked, never blocking. Routes half-formed thoughts back to the vault.
